History

Basswalk Records (U.S.A.), was founded April 10th, 2002 by Miami's music executive; Toby Holguin under the distribution of Inversus Records.

From its beginnings, it managed to grow rapidly into the scene by Basswalk Records having participation in the Ultra Music Festival, Miami editions 3, 4, and 5 with their own stage. It has been distributed by the Universal/Vivendi Music Group in territories like Brazil and Mexico. Nowadays, after working many years with HousePlanet.uk, it has started a new cycle as a Beatport exclusive label through Baseware Distribution and has founded a sister label under the name of Low Life Records, currently distributed by Brazil Deluxe. In 2013, Toby and the chilean Aldo Cadiz decided to create Badbarbie Records.

In 10 years of great diverse underground music, many distinguished artists of the likes of; Stefano Noferini, Pig & Dan, Marcello Azevedo, Nexus DJ & Mon, Shmuel Flash, Angel Moraes, Austin Leeds, Benz & MD, Chus & Ceballos, Toby Holguin, Ashbury & Haight, Raya & Manku, Ronan Portela, Elio Riso, DJ Moffus - Soundmodul - Francesco Parla, Erick E., Habersham, Ivan Miranda, Jay Tripwire, Lemon 8, Liam Shachar, Luke Fair, Neil Kolo, Oliver Berger, Pole Folder, Remy Unger, Kabale Und Liebe, Phillip O, Diego Astaiza, Manuel Belgrano, and Andre Mustache have redefined the label to its authenticity, well rooted where it all began.

Basswalk is also involved in night club projects like La Sala, La Pola, and currently Blackdoor Club where Basswalk has th

ere label night as well as Toby's worldwide night "From the INside OUT".

Español: Basswalk Records (Estados Unidos), fue fundada el 10 de abril de 2002 por el músico ejecutivo de Miami, Toby Holguin; con la distribución de Inversus Records.

Desde sus comienzos, se las arregló para crecer rápidamente en la escena mediante la participación de Basswalk Records con su propio escenario en el Ultra Music Festival, ediciones Miami 3, 4, y 5. Este ha sido distribuido por Universal/Vivendi Music Group en territorios como Brasil y México. Hoy en día, después de haber trabajado muchos años con HousePlanet.uk, Basswalk ha iniciado un nuevo ciclo como un sello exclusivo de Beatport a través de Baseware Distribution y ha fundado un sello hermano bajo el nombre de Low Life Records, actualmente distribuidos por Brazil Deluxe.

En 10 años de grandiosa y diversa música alternativa (“underground”), muchos destacados artistas de la talla de Stefano Noferini, Pig & Dan, Marcello Azevedo, Nexus DJ & Mon, Shmuel Flash, Ángel Moraes, Austin Leeds, Benz & MD, Chus & Ceballos, Toby Holguin, Ashbury & Haight, Raya & Manku, Ronan Portela, Elio Riso, DJ Moffus - Soundmodul – Francesco Parla, Erick E., Habersham, Iván Miranda, Jay Tripwire, Lemon 8, Liam Shachar, Luke Fair, Neil Kolo, Oliver Berger, Pole Folder, Remy Unger, Kabale Und Liebe, Phillip O, Diego Astaiza, Manuel Belgrano, y André Mustache, han redefinido el sello en toda su autenticidad, bien fundamentado y enraizado en donde todo comenzó.

Basswalk también está involucrado en proyectos de clubes nocturnos como La Sala, La Pola, y en la actualidad con el Club Cerati, en donde Basswalk tiene su noche de etiqueta, así como la noche mundial de Toby "From the INside OUT”.

Studio Room

Bass Walk Studios offers TDM Protools, Reason, and Logic on Envision double screens run by a G4 Mac / G5 Mac. Hardware compressors in the studio include the ISA 430 Focusrite and the Platinum Penta Focusrite.

Killer Synths include Minimoog-Voyager, Waldorf Q, Orbit 3, Proteus 2000, Roland JP-8000, Oxygen 8, Micro Korg-Synthetizer Vocoder. many virtual synths, and the TDM plug-ins. Speakers are powered by Yamaha.

Recording Room

Bass Walk Studios is equipped with the finest isolation and noise reduction components. Mics include the Neumann TLM 103, Senheiser MD 421, Audio Technica 4033a, Senheiser 441U3, and Audio Technica AP 901. The digital console is the Yamaha O2R 24 Digital 24 Channel mixer. Marshall JCM 800 Series to record the variety of Fender, Gibson, and Washburn guitars. Electric Bass is model Ibanez SD-GR. Percussion instruments are Matador Congas and bongos, plus many percussion effects. Live recording of dj's mixes into the Protools for professional edited mixes.
